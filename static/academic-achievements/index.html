Academic Achievements

Awards and Recognition • May 6, 2021
  • IOQP and IOQA 2020-21 (Part I), PRMO qualified.
  • KVPY Fellowship awarded for securing All India Rank 136 in KVPY SA examination conducted by IISc Bangalore in 2019.
  • Stood First in the World in the International Youth Math Challenge (IYMC 2020), one of the biggest online math competition for students from all around the world.
  • Secured All India Merit Rank 3 in Indian Certificate of Secondary Education ICSE Board Class X Examination -2019 conducted by the Council for Indian School Certificate Examinations (CISCE) New Delhi.
  • GOOGLE CLOUD PLATFORM FUNDAMENTALS: CORE INFRASTRUCTURE: An online non-credit course authorized by Google Cloud and offered through Coursera
  • PHYSICS OF SEMICONDUCTORS: An online course organised by Centre for Development of Technical Education and INDIAN INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY -IIT KANPUR.
  • INTRODUCTION TO LIGHT COLOUR AND LIFE: An online course on Physics organised by KOREA ADVANCED INSTITIUTE OF TECHNOLOGY.
